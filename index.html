Experience

Urbanist

I believe the shape and function of cities and neighborhoods should let us live our lives to the fullest. For me, this means ditching the car as much as possible, not so easy in a city like Tampa. I'm not in spandex, just a guy getting around by bike, engineering as many car-less travel as possible.
