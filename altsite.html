Experience

My nine years in journalism span the media spectrum.

In South Louisiana, I told stories of residents struggling in the aftermath of the Deepwater Horizon oil spill and hurricanes Gustav and Ike.

As a reporter in central Florida, I turned my focus to stories that spoke to a place's unique identity: A young boy's struggle after a deadly gang shooting, the community's reaction to new technology implemented on school buses, a family's hopes tied to a cornfield growing in the Green Swamp.

As a digital editor with ABC Action News, I cover breaking news for the station's website and mobile app. I also helm the station's social media and create video content designed specifically for a digital audience. My web development skillset is constantly growing (I designed and built the website you're reading).
